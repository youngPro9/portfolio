The Obstacle Arm

C, Python, FreeRTOS

The Obstacle Arm is the Rover with the robotic arm and IR sensor attached to its top and front respectively. The Rover follows the path retrieved from server. While it's in operation, the IR sensor sends "detected" signal to the Rover if anything is detected in the front within 5 cm distance. Once the Rover receives the "detected" signal, it stops and communicates with server that sends "move obstalce" signal to the robotic arm. The server, then, waits for the "complete" message from the arm. After "complete" message's retrieved, server keeps sending the operation commands to the Rover to make it follow the path.
